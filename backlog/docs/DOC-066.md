## Description

Precision follow-up surfaced during `/review` of the ingestion-auth batch (DOC-004 + DOC-012, both accepted 2026-04-24). Two small issues to fold into a future touch on these pages:

1. **Phrasing precision on the sibling-path table row** (`enable-security/README.md:20`). Today's row reads:

   > | All other `/ingestion/*` paths … | — | — | **Unauthenticated in all configurations.** The filters above match exact path patterns; they do not cover sibling paths. |

   The claim "in all configurations" is accurate under `auth.type` ∈ {`DISABLED`, `OAUTH2`, `LDAP`} (all three use `SecurityConstants.WHITELIST_PATHS`, which carries `/ingestion/**` through the permit-all branch). Under `auth.type=LOGIN_FORM`, `LoginFormSecurityConfiguration.java:49-57` whitelists only `{/actuator/health, /favicon.ico, /ingestion/entities, /ingestion/datasources, /api/slack/events}` and sends everything else (`/**`) through `.pathMatchers("/**").authenticated()` — so sibling ingestion paths like `/ingestion/alert/alertmanager`, `/ingestion/entities/degs/children`, `/ingestion/entities/datasets/stats` are session-gated under LOGIN_FORM, not unauthenticated.

   Operational impact is nil (LOGIN_FORM is dev-only per `application.yml:36` and DOC-012 explicitly warns against it in production), but the phrasing is imprecise. Amend the cell to scope the claim to the production-path auth modes and note the LOGIN_FORM session-gating.

2. **Footer citation augmentation** on the two commits that shipped DOC-004 + DOC-012 (`fb62950`, `3e1b8c8`). Both footers are correct but narrow:

   - DOC-004 (`fb62950`) `Sources:` lists `IngestionDataEntitiesFilter.java:19-31` (class header + constructor) but the token-validation body logic at `:43-60` is what enforces the "validates the token against the datasource's stored token (falls back to the collector's token)" claim on `README.md:19`. Widen to `:19-31,43-60` in the next amendment.
   - DOC-004 claim at `README.md:26` ("`odd-collector-sdk` already attach[es] `Authorization: Bearer <token>`") is verified by `odd-collectors/odd-collector-sdk/odd_collector_sdk/api/datasource_api.py:21-24` (`self.headers = {"Authorization": f"Bearer {token}"}`) + lines 31-36, 53-58. Add as `Cross-repo:` citation.
   - DOC-012 (`3e1b8c8`) `Sources:` lists the config declaration + Spring Boot relaxed-binding rule but omits the sole `@Value` consumer `LoginFormSecurityConfiguration.java:70` (and the ADMIN-privileges enforcement at line 81, `grantedAuthorityExtractor.getAuthorities(true)`). Add as `Config-consumer:` citations so future auditors don't have to re-derive.
